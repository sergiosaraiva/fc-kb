## 1.9 Consequences of a first consolidation for subsidiaries

With respect to the group companies, the consolidation department plays a triple role of information, planning and training.

The companies themselves will have to adapt their accounting system in order to comply with the parent company requirements in the shortest time with a minimum of efforts and a maximum of quality.
