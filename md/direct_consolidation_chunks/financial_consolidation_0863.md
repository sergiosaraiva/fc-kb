### What will be the banks position?

The experience shows that the fact company A doesn't consolidate any more is very often perceived as a problem by the banks. They are used to analyse figures and ratios in relation with existing contracts signed with company A.

Moreover, new assets and new liabilities in company P accounts will probably need some additional reviews from the banks. This to say that, whatever is the choice, the Bankers will probably ask to receive "group A" consolidated accounts.
