### Dividends paid to indirect  3rd  Parties

The problem we want to highlight happens in this group structure where we suppose company B is paying a dividend of 100.

The  3rd  Parties percentage of B is the addition of direct  3rd  Parties for  40%  and indirect  3rd  Parties, through A for  12% = 20% \times 60%  in B.

We say that the indirect  3rd  Parties percentage is then  52% .
