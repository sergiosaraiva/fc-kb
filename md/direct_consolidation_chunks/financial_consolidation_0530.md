# Chunk 530: Consolidated flows

## Context

This section covers Consolidated flows.

## Content

Adjustment J1: The cash flow calculation takes into account the gains on tangible assets disposals but ignores the consolidated gain on  20%  A shares disposal for 26. This amount is not present because financial investments are not in the balance sheet.

Adjustment 12: For the same reason, the acquisition price of company B must be added

Adjustment 13: And also the disposal price of the  20%  A shares

Adjustment J4: Finally, the amount of cash subscribed by the  3rd  Parties to the increase of capital of company B is not there either because the capital is eliminated in the consolidation process. To be added.

Notice that all these adjustments have an impact on the cash line of the cash flow statement.
