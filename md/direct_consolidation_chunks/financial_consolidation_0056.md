When determining the power of control, one should take into account two elements:

- Shares directly held by the parent company and  
- Shares indirectly held by the parent, i.e. via subsidiaries. In this case, the percentage of control of the subsidiary adds to that of the parent.

The word 'control' is very important because there are multiple levels of control, and the level of control will determine how to consolidate. There are three different situations of controls that will induce the related consolidation method.
