## 4.2 Absorption of a company

It is important to make a clear distinction between an absorption of a company and the merge of two companies.

The absorption relies on the single fact that the absorbing company owns  100%  of the shares of the company that will be absorbed.

A merge of two (or more) companies can result in the ownership by different shareholders at any percentage. The next section will analyze a merge of two companies.
