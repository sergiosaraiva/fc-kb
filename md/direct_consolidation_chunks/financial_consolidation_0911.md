## 2.2 Content

A consolidation bundle generally presents a tree structure consisting in about five different levels and a set of validation rules.

#### Level 1

This level concerns some general information

The current consolidation period  
The reference consolidation period

Company name and additional coordinates

Company currency  
- Correspondent name and additional coordinates  
Optionally, the CFO approval  
Optionally, the Auditors approval

#### Level 2

This level usually contains balance sheet, P&L closing amounts of the reference period (read only) and the current year amounts.
