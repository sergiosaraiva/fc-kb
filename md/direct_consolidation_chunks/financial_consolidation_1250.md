## Answer 1.01

Number of voting rights owned is  1800 = 800^{*}1 + 200^{*}0 + 500^{*}2.

Number of voting rights issued is  3000 = 1000 * 1 + 1000 * 0 + 1000 * 2 .

The control percentage is  60% = 1800 / 3000  with a global integration method.
