## Content

### Answer 2.08

The amount of 150 in the transfer column has nothing to do with dividends or interim dividends. We have seen several times that this column was used mainly for two reasons, either a decrease in percentage in company A or some elimination of group profit via the Reserves account.

The option d is not correct.
