## Question 1.07

The structure of this group remains unchanged in Year 1 and Year 2.

Company B is a foreign company with currency CUR.

At the end of Year 1, company B decides to pay dividends of 1000 CUR and local laws require company B to withhold a tax of  25%  on these dividends.

Company A receives a cash amount of 525 CUR = 70% * [75% * 1000 CUR] which corresponds to 470 EUR on the Bank receipt and book for that amount on the Financial income account.
