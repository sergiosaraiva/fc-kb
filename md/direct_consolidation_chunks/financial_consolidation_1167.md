## Dividend Flow Context (Question 1.07):
This structure is used to calculate dividend elimination with withholding tax:
- B (foreign company) pays dividends of 1000 CUR at end of Year 1
- Local laws require 25% withholding tax on dividends
- A receives: 525 CUR = 70% × [75% × 1000 CUR]
- Converted to EUR: 470 EUR (on Financial income account)
- Exchange rates: Year 1 = 0.8, Year 2 = 0.7
