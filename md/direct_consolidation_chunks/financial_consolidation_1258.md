### Answer 1.09

The cash flow is equal to the consolidated result  +  non cash charges - non cash incomes.

From our situation, we get a cash flow of  \(550 = 300 + 200 + 100 + (50)\) , considering the only non cash items are "Depreciations", 'Provisions" and 'Profit from equity method company".
