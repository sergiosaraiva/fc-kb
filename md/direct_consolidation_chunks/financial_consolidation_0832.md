## Content

### Comment 2

Notice also that it is important to know which accounts are considered as part of the equity of the company. Some accounts like "Currency translation adjustments" (CTA), "Investments grants" (if not IFRS), "Revaluation reserves", ... should be included in the equity. The CTA, for instance, gives then a present value instead of an historical value.
