## Prepare all events having an impact on consolidated accounts

All along the period to consolidate, stay informed of all events impacting the consolidation.

The list of these events can be rather long and we think about

New company acquisition  
- Disposal of a company  
All shares transactions (acquisitions and disposals) concerning consolidated companies
