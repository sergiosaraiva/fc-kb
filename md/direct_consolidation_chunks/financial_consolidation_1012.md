## ALPHA - Column (4)

Initially, we had a gross goodwill of 700 corresponding to the  70%  acquisition of GAMMA. In January this year,


|   | Debit | Credit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Goodwill (gross val.) | 300 |   |
| Fin.invest./GAMMA |   | 300 |


ALPHA has sold  40%  of these shares, remaining with a participation of  30% . The remaining gross goodwill kept in the consolidation is than  300 = 700 \times [30% / 70%] .
